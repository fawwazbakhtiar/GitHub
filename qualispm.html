SPM

Akedemi Tahfiz Nurid Al-Ilmi

"Passed SPM Even Though Studied
for Only 2 Years."

After completing my studies at the Maahad level, I continued my studies to the next level, which is SPM. Nurid Al-Ilmi Tahfiz Academy, or its acronym ATNA, has witnessed the arduous journey I undertook to obtain an SPM certificate. With the Covid-19 pandemic that struck at that time, I only had 2 years to catch up on all the learning before facing the exams. A journey that was not easy with limited time, yet successfully navigated thanks to patience and prayer.

The exams ended, and I managed to get 4As and 4Bs in the SPM. It's not as impressive as others, but I am still grateful for the results obtained. As a result, I successfully received several offers to continue my studies in a Diploma program at the University, but I chose to continue my studies at the STPM level.
