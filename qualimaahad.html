Maahad

Maahad Tahfiz Al-Fateh

"Hafiz at The
Age of 15."

Maahad Tahfiz Al-Fateh, located on Jalan Kebun, Shah Alam, was the first place for me to start my journey of seeking knowledge at the secondary level. Fun fact, I only attended primary school until I was 11 years old and started entering Tahfiz when I turned 12. When I was in Tahfiz, I successfully completed the Quran and became a Hafiz at the age of 15. Indirectly, that became my last year at the Tahfiz. Life in the dormitory was not as beautiful as imagined, but the bittersweet memories created back then remain until today.

At this stage, there are various things that have shaped me into who I am today. Undeniably, life in Tahfiz has made me a disciplined, responsible, time-conscious, and respectful person. In my personal opinion, I have never regretted entering Tahfiz because it has taught me many things that I might not have learned elsewhere.
